# 1. Purpose

This roadmap outlines the planned development phases for the AI Company Brain platform. It defines the project's short-term and long-term goals, major milestones, feature releases, and future vision.

The roadmap provides guidance for developers, stakeholders, and future contributors while ensuring structured product growth.

---

# 2. Product Vision

Build an AI-powered knowledge management platform where companies can securely store, organize, search, and retrieve their organizational knowledge using Artificial Intelligence.

The platform should become the single source of truth for company information.

# 7. Risks

Potential project risks include:

- AI response quality
- Large document processing
- Scaling challenges
- Infrastructure costs
- Data privacy
- Security vulnerabilities
- Third-party API limitations

Mitigation strategies include continuous testing, monitoring, regular security audits, and scalable cloud infrastructure.

---

# 8. Long-Term Vision

The long-term goal is to transform AI Company Brain into a complete enterprise knowledge platform that enables organizations to securely manage information, automate workflows, and enhance productivity through Artificial Intelligence.

The platform aims to become a trusted AI-powered workspace for businesses worldwide.

---

# 9. Conclusion

This roadmap provides a structured plan for the evolution of AI Company Brain from documentation and MVP development to a scalable enterprise SaaS platform. By following these phases, the project will deliver incremental value while maintaining quality, security, and scalability.
