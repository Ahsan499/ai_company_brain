# Executive Summary

AI Company Brain is an AI-powered enterprise knowledge platform designed to centralize organizational knowledge.

The platform helps companies preserve information, improve productivity, accelerate onboarding, and provide trusted AI-powered answers from internal company data.

---

# Business Problem

Organizations store information in many disconnected systems.

Examples include:

- GitHub
- Notion
- Google Drive
- Slack
- Jira
- Emails
- PDFs
- Meeting Notes

Employees waste hours searching for information.

Knowledge is lost when employees resign.

Documentation becomes outdated.

New developers require weeks to understand projects.

---

# Business Opportunity

The rapid adoption of Artificial Intelligence has created demand for intelligent enterprise assistants.

Companies increasingly require:

- Internal AI Chat
- Knowledge Search
- Documentation AI
- Developer Assistant
- Project Understanding
- AI-powered onboarding

The enterprise AI market is expected to continue growing over the coming years, making this an attractive opportunity.
