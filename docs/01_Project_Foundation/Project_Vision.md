# Vision Statement

AI Company Brain is an enterprise AI platform that acts as the digital brain of an organization.

Instead of simply storing files, the platform understands company knowledge, software projects, technical documentation, business processes, and organizational decisions.

The objective is to help organizations preserve knowledge, improve productivity, reduce onboarding time, and enable employees to quickly access trusted information.

---

# Why Are We Building This Product?

Modern software companies lose valuable time because important information is scattered across different platforms.

Developers spend hours searching for documentation.

Project managers search through old chats.

New employees require weeks to understand the system.

When experienced employees leave the company, much of the company's knowledge leaves with them.

There is no intelligent centralized platform that truly understands the company's knowledge.

AI Company Brain aims to solve this problem.

---

# Problem Statement

Organizations use many different tools every day.

Examples include:

- GitHub
- Google Drive
- Notion
- Jira
- Slack
- Microsoft Teams
- PDFs
- Word Documents
- Internal Wikis
- Meeting Notes
- Emails

Because information is distributed across multiple systems:

- Knowledge becomes difficult to find.
- Teams duplicate work.
- Documentation becomes outdated.
- Productivity decreases.
- New employee onboarding becomes slow.
- Organizational knowledge is lost.

---

# Proposed Solution

Build an AI-powered knowledge platform capable of:

- Understanding company documentation.
- Reading software repositories.
- Learning from project documents.
- Answering technical questions.
- Explaining source code.
- Preserving organizational knowledge.
- Providing trusted answers with references.

---

# Mission

Build the world's most trusted AI knowledge platform for software companies.

---

# Long-Term Vision

AI Company Brain will eventually become the operating system for organizational knowledge.

Future capabilities may include:

- AI Developer Assistant
- AI Project Manager
- AI Documentation Generator
- AI Release Notes Generator
- AI Bug Investigation Assistant
- AI Architecture Assistant
- AI Business Reports
- AI Workflow Automation

Version 1 will focus only on building a reliable knowledge platform.

# Initial MVP Scope

Version 1 will include:

- Secure User Authentication
- User Roles & Permissions
- Upload Documents
- GitHub Repository Synchronization
- AI Chat
- Knowledge Search
- Source References
- Admin Dashboard

---

# Out of Scope

The following features will NOT be included in Version 1:

- CRM
- HR Management
- Finance
- Payroll
- Workflow Builder
- AI Automation
- AI Agents
- Email Automation
- WhatsApp Integration

These features may be added in future versions.

---

# Product Principles

Every decision made in this project must follow these principles.

1. Solve real business problems.
2. Keep the architecture clean.
3. Prioritize security.
4. Build scalable systems.
5. Document every important decision.
6. Quality is more important than speed.
7. Simplicity before complexity.

---

# Success Metrics

Version 1 will be considered successful if:

- Employees find information within seconds.
- New developer onboarding time is significantly reduced.
- Company knowledge is preserved.
- Documentation becomes searchable.
- Developers trust AI responses.
- Companies begin using the platform daily.
